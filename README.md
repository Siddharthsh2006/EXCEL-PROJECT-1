This project presents an interactive business intelligence dashboard created to analyze and visualize the annual sales performance of Vrinda Store. The goal is to provide a clear, data-driven overview of key performance indicators (KPIs) to inform strategic decision-making in sales, marketing, and operations.

Key Features & Insights:
Performance Trends: Visualizes monthly orders and sales revenue, highlighting the strong peak in the first half of the year and the concerning decline in the second half (H2).

Customer Segmentation: Breaks down sales by Gender (64% Women) and analyzes order distribution across Adult, Senior, and Teenager age groups.

Geographical Focus: Identifies the Top 5 highest-performing states by sales amount (led by Maharashtra and Karnataka).

Channel Effectiveness: Measures the share of orders across major platforms, revealing a high dependency on Amazon (35%) and Flipkart (22%).

Operational Health: Displays order status, showing a robust 92% delivery rate and a low overall return/cancellation rate (8%).

Project Outcome:
The analysis pinpoints critical areas for management focus, specifically the need to stabilize H2 sales and diversify the high-risk channel mix for sustainable growth.

Technologies Used: MS Excel
